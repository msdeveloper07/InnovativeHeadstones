General Questions

Innovative Headstones does not install headstones. We manufacture and ship your stone to the residence, business or cemetery that you specify in your order. For orders larger than flat 24″x12″x4″ (excluding Imperial Black), delivery drivers are NOT required to unload the stone from the truck. Someone MUST be present during delivery to sign for the stone and unload it from the truck. Failure to comply may result in a failed delivery attempt and additional charges being billed to the purchaser.

If the cemetery you have selected does not install the headstone there are usually local monument companies that can be contracted to do the installation work. Most cemeteries require the family prior to the headstone being shipped pay any setting fees. It is the purchaser’s responsibility to pay all fees prior to shipment. Failure to pay applicable fees may result in the rejection of the delivery of your headstone.

Most cemeteries will have a contact for doing final death dates. If the Cemetery does not, then a local Monument Company will.

Contact the cemetery to inquire about specifications and installation before placing an order. Specifications may include size, color and material of the Headstone. CLICK HERE to read more about questions to ask your cemetery.

Most cemeteries adhere to certain specifications that may include size, color and material of the Headstone. You should always check with your cemetery before placing an order.

The Innovative Headstones Online Headstone Designer (not available in all sizes and styles at this time) will show you a live preview of the Headstone all the way through the design process. Once you finalize your order, you will also receive an email confirmation with a link to view your final design. All orders placed through our Catalog will receive a proof from one of our designers for your review and approval prior to the Headstone being made.

  • Flat Markers from 12″ x 8″ – 24″ x 12″ are shipped via FedEx Ground Delivery
  • Larger headstones and monuments are shipped on a pallet through a trucking company due to their size and weight.
  • Shipping times vary depending on shipping destination. Tracking information will be emailed to you once your order ships.

Flat Headstones do not include a base. Each flat granite Headstone is a single piece with no base required.

  • During the ordering process, you select the artwork and text that will be applied to your Headstone.
  • Your selected artwork and text is then sandblasted into the granite.
  • A coat of paint is applied to the sandblasted areas on the marker. All grey markers will be painted black and all other color markers will be painted white.

Orders for in stock items will ship 4 weeks after the approval of the design. All orders received after hours will begin production the next business day.

Shipping times vary based on destination. In general orders take 5-7 business days to arrive.

Several terms are used to refer to Headstones. These include headstone, gravestone, marker, flat marker, grave marker, grass marker, memorial, monument and tombstone.

Headstones weights vary depending on size, thickness and color. For example our most common Headstone is a 24″ x 12″ x 3″ in Grey. Headstone weights range from 86 lbs. to over 1,000 pounds in some of the larger styles.
